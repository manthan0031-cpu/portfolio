Terra

Verde

Role: Design & Development Deliverables: Shopify Theme, Configurator
01 / Overview

The Challenge

Terra Verde needed a digital storefront that mirrored their commitment to sustainability — not just in product, but in experience. Every pixel had to whisper "earth-first" while converting browsers into believers.

The challenge was to build a Shopify experience that felt bespoke, not templated. A custom product configurator, seamless checkout, and editorial-grade photography integration were non-negotiables.

01 / Custom Product Configurator

Custom Product Configurator

A real-time product customization engine built in Liquid and JavaScript. Customers can select materials, colors, and engravings with live pricing updates and 3D preview rendering.

  • Real-time price calculation
  • 3D product preview
  • Material swatch interactions
Page 03
02 / Sustainable Storytelling

Sustainable Storytelling

Every product page tells the full story — from raw material sourcing to carbon footprint. Interactive timelines and impact calculators turn purchasing into an act of conscious choice.

  • Interactive supply chain map
  • Carbon footprint calculator
  • Impact storytelling cards

03 / Custom Shopify Theme

Custom Shopify Theme

A ground-up Shopify 2.0 theme built with Liquid, JSON templates, and Sections architecture. Zero reliance on third-party apps — every feature is native, performant, and maintainable.

  • Shopify 2.0 Sections architecture
  • JSON template system
  • App-free feature stack
